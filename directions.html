Where to find the Venue

Hartlebury Castle is situated approximately 5 miles south of Kidderminster and 8 miles north of Worcester, just off the A449 main road.

Follow the signs reading Hartlebury Castle into the village of Hartlebury.

Public Transport

There are a number of taxi companies in Kidderminster who operate in the Hartlebury area. We recommend booking taxis in advance.

Hartlebury Bus Stop is a 15-20 minute walk and Hartlebury Railway Station is a 20-30 minute walk from the Castle.
Please check public transport timetables for more details. Larger bus and railway stations are located in Kidderminster, approximately 4 miles from the Castle.

Car Parking

Free Parking is available on site.
